Waste Classification Gallery

Organic

Food scraps, yard waste, and other biodegradable materials. Proper composting reduces landfill methane emissions.

Plastic

Bottles, containers, and packaging. Recycling plastic prevents microplastic pollution in our oceans and soil.

Metal

Cans, foil, and scrap metal. Recycling metals saves significant energy compared to mining raw materials.

E-Waste

Electronics, batteries, and appliances. Special handling prevents toxic heavy metals from leaching into groundwater.

Hazardous

Chemicals, paint, and medical waste. Requires professional disposal to prevent severe environmental and health risks.
